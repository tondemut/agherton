Mission + Market is available for special occasions, meetings and private events.  Our private dining room features a projector and full audio/visual set up, a curated wine list specifically for events and culinary options ranging from passed canapés and hors d’oeuvres to multi-course dining experiences. The area also provides access to our patio which allows for indoor/outdoor event capabilities. Private dining at Mission + Market is anywhere from 20-75 guests, after 80 guests we recommend a full buyout.  All of us here at Mission + Market look forward to customizing an incredible private dining experience with you soon!
